## Key Status Effects

### Iron Maiden
- Gain +4 Aggro to the leftmost Slot
- **Turn Start:** Gain 1 Protection, 5 Defense Level Up, and Shield HP equal to 5% of this unit's current HP
- When hit by the last Coin of an attacker, deal Pierce damage equal to 20% of said Coin's damage and inflict +2 Bleed Count (max once per Skill, 3 times per turn. Max damage per turn: 60)

### The Self Unbound — Flow State
- Min & Max Speed +2
- Gain a new set of Base Skills
- Deal +20% damage with Base Attack Skills to enemies whose Speed is slower than this unit

### Corpus Ingredient (Unique Charge)
- Max Count: 20
- Interacts the same way as normal Charge does to effects that raise or reduce Charge Potency and Count
- **Turn End:** Lose 1 Count

### Artwork: Fascia
Apply the following effects to this unit's Base Attack Skills and Clashable Counter Skill based on Corpus Ingredient Potency:
- **1+:** Base Power +1
- **2+:** If the enemy has Bleed or 'Unique Bleed', deal +10% damage
- **3+:** Inflict +1 more Bleed Potency and Count
- **4+:** Base Power +1
