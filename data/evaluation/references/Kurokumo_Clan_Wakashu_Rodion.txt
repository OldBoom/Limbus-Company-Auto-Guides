Kurokumo Clan Wakashu Rodion is a Damage Dealer who stacks Poise for Coin Power bonuses and converts Critical Hits on Bleed targets into massive Bleed infliction. Skill 1 gains 4 Poise on hit and unlocks Coin Power +2 at 4+ Poise; Skill 2 builds both Poise and Bleed simultaneously, with a Poise Count extension on use. Skill 3 (Sky-clearing Cut) doubles its Critical Hit chance when the target has 6+ Bleed, deals +100% Critical damage at 10+ Poise, and on crit inflicts 10 Bleed — so stack Poise with Skill 1 and Skill 2 first, then commit Skill 3 when the target already has Bleed and your Poise is at 10+. The Combat Passive (Dark Cloud Blade) converts Counter uses into Skill 3 when she has 5+ Poise, giving a second window per turn to proc the 10 Bleed critical.
