Wild Hunt Heathcliff is a Sinking Damage Dealer who stacks Coffin through Skill 3 use and kills to amplify Memorial Procession and Requiem (+12% damage per Coffin, up to 120%), while building Sinking and Impending Ruin on enemies. Dullahan—applied at Combat Start via Counter when absent—mounts at Turn Start and swaps Skill 3 to Lament, Mourn, and Despair, a high Base Power finisher that scales off Coffin, Dullahan, missing SP, and enemy Sinking. Call of the Erlking spends 2 Coffin when an ally would die to apply Wild Hunt (survive at 1 HP, revive at Turn End) and buffs that ally, while adding +1% damage per Sinking on target for Heathcliff's own hits. Defense usage: **Counter: O Dullahan…!** grants Dullahan next turn at Combat Start when absent; with Dullahan and 15+ SP it fires **Lament, Mourn, and Despair** as a Counter once per turn for burst without spending the rotation's Skill 3. Clash Wins on the Counter stack Offense Level for the following turn — use it to set up Dullahan before mounting and committing the finisher. Support Passive (Endless Lamentation) amplifies your highest-SP ally against sub-0 SP targets and adds Sinking Potency/Count on their attack skills. Manage SP around Dullahan mounting and stack Coffin before committing the finisher slot each rotation.
