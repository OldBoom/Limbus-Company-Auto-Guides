Edgar Family Chief Butler Ryōshū is a Damage Dealer who marks targets with B.M. and then eliminates that debuff on Critical Hits for Poise-scaled burst damage. All skills gain Clash Power +1 per 2 Speed difference (max 3) when faster than the target; Skill 2 can spend 6 Poise Count at Combat Start for +2 Atk Weight; Skill 3 (Receiving Arts 2: S.Y.N.C.) grants +7 Poise Count on use, inflicts Bind and raises Stagger Threshold on hit, deals +40% damage on Crit against Staggered targets, and on Kill reuses itself against a random enemy that has B.M. The Combat Passive (Clearing Household Chores) grants Poise Count and Haste on every hit against a B.M. target and, on a Critical Hit, eliminates B.M. entirely while dealing +10% per Speed difference (max 60%) — prioritise outpacing targets to convert accumulated Poise into crit damage.
