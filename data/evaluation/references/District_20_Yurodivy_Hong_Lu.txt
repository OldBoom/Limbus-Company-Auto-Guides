District 20 Yurodivy Hong Lu is a Tremor Burst Damage Dealer who builds Tremor Count on self to fuel escalating Coin Power on Skill 3 (You're the Culprit!). Skill 1 and Skill 2 apply Tremor stacks and build self Tremor Count; Skill 2 also triggers Tremor Burst on the third Coin if the target has 7+ Tremor, and Skill 3 consumes 10 or 15 Tremor Count on use to gain Coin Power +2 or +3, with coins triggering Amplitude Entanglement into Tremor — Reverb and additional Bursts. The Combat Passive (Vibroweld Morph-combat Cane) stacks up to 3 Damage Up per turn based on how many Tremor Bursts were triggered, rewarding aggressive Burst-chaining rotations. His Support Passive (In the Culprit's Shoes) gives the first-deployed ally a 25% chance to trigger an extra Tremor Burst on each Burst trigger, making him a strong support pick in Tremor compositions.
