Lobotomy E.G.O::The Sword Sharpened with Tears Rodion is a Minus Coin Damage Dealer who must reach negative SP to unlock her full kit. At 0+ SP she runs Blessing Plus Coin skills (The Knight's Sanctuary, With the Power of Justice, Arcana Pierce); below 0 SP she switches to Despair Minus Coin alternates (Faded Faith, With the Tear-sharpened Sword, Arcana: Tear and Pierce) with far higher Base Power and up to +20% damage from missing SP. Plan several early turns on Guard (The Knight's Faith) or Counter (Weathered Pride) — both have Unbreakable Coins, grant Deep Tears on use, and at Turn End lose 15 SP per Tear-sharpened stack gained (up to 3), accelerating the drop into Despair. Skill 3 consumes up to 20 Deep Tears for +1.5% per stack in Blessing and +3% in Despair; at -15 SP the support passive adds Final Power to allies' Minus Coin skills, and at -30 SP adds +15% damage as well. Tear-sharpened also enables a coin reuse on Despair Skill 3's final coin.
