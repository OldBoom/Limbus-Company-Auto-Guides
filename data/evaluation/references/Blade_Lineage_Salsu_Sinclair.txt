Blade Lineage Salsu Sinclair is a high-burst Poise Damage Dealer whose Skill 3 (To Claim Their Bones) deals +150% Critical Damage at 10+ Poise Count. Skill 1 builds Poise Count on heads hits and grants Coin Power +1 at 3+ Poise Count; Skill 2 generates Poise on hit and — crucially — on Clash Win does not spend Poise Count when landing critical attacks, allowing the count to persist into Skill 3. The Counter (Newsprung Counter) grants +3 Poise Count and 1 Damage Up, making it a meaningful resource-builder rather than a pure defensive tool. The Support Passive (Slayer) gives the last Coin of the last skill in a chain +20% damage and passively feeds her Poise Count whenever any other ally gains Poise Count, making her the premier carry of Blade Lineage compositions.
