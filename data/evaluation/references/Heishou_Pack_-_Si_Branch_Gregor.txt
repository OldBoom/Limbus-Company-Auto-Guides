Heishou Pack - Si Branch Gregor is a Poise and Rupture hybrid Damage Dealer who scales Coin Power from the combined total of Poise on self and Rupture on target. Skill 1 and Skill 2 inflict Rupture and build both Poise Count and Rupture Count on Clash Win; Skill 2's third Coin spends 10 Poise to Reuse the Coin once and applies the unique Deathrite — Venom — debuff. Skill 3 (Serpentshear Puncturing Arm) gains 3 Serpent Arm on use — which improves Coin Power scaling — and deals up to +150% damage on a single coin based on the Poise + Rupture sum, while each coin hit reduces Deathrite — Venom — and inflicts it on the target. **Guard: Reversal Kick** grants 3 Serpent Arm at Combat Start — use the defense slot to stack Serpent Arm before committing Skill 3. The Combat Passive (Heishou Mutation [Si]) adds +5% damage per Serpent Arm (max 15%) against targets that have Deathrite — Venom —, and a second passive ensures that if Gregor dies during the encounter, the substitute identity gains Poise each turn at Combat Start.
