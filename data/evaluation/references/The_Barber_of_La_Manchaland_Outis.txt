The Barber of La Manchaland Outis is a Damage Dealer who converts the shared Bloodfeast resource into Blood-tinged Scissorblades to power a high-multiplier Skill 3 finisher. Skills 1 and 2 consume Bloodfeast to generate Scissorblades (1 per 8 or 6 Bloodfeast consumed), and Skill 2 applies the Sewing Target debuff to the main target on the final coin; Skill 3 (I'll Make You a New Dress!) deals +(Scissorblades × 3)% damage at 10+ stacks (max 57%) or +(Scissorblades × 4)% at 20+ (max 120%), and on killing a Sewing Target refunds Bloodfeast and Scissorblades to sustain the loop. Her Support Passive (WRONG COSTUME!!!) grants Clash Power +1 against Sewing Target enemies and adds flat damage bonuses based on total Bloodfeast consumed this encounter and current Bleed on the target.
