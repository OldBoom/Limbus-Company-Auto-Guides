The Ring Fauvist Student Meursault is a Status Specialist and Damage Dealer who spreads both Bleed and Sinking to maximise multi-negative-effect damage scaling. Skills 1 and 2 deal +5% damage per negative effect type on target (max 15%/20%) and gain Coin Power from the combined total of (Bleed + Sinking) on target; the Combat Passive applies 1 Mauled Color — Rouge — on every Base Skill hit and, after 10+ have been inflicted total, grants Le Fauvisme — Masque de Chien, which strengthens the Counter. Skill 3 (Le Fauvisme — Reveal Submission) scales to +10% per negative effect type (max 60%), gains Final Power +1 per 4 (Bleed + Sinking, max 4), and when below 50% HP or when the target has 5+ Mauled Color, activates an additional final coin hit with all coins converted to Unbreakable.
