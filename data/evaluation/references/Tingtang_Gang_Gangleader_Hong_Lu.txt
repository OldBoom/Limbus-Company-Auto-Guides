Tingtang Gang Gangleader Hong Lu is a Damage Dealer who relies on Heads coin flips and Bleed stacks to burst down targets, with an On Kill chain on Skill 3. Skill 1 (Throat Slit) inflicts 4 Bleed on Heads Hit; Skill 2 (Shank) deals +20% damage when the target has 7+ Bleed, and its final coin can reuse itself if the target survives at below 25% HP. Skill 3 (Mutilate) deals +100% damage on the Reuse Heads Hit, adds +50% if the target has 10+ Bleed, and fires again immediately on any kill — enabling chain-kill turns. His Combat Passive (Gambit) grants the ally with the most SP +20% damage on Heads Coins, and the Support Passive (Gaiety) heals SP equal to twice his Gluttony Resonance next turn.
