Kurokumo Clan Wakashu Ryōshū is a Damage Dealer who scales all three base skills off Bleed Count — not Bleed Potency — on the target. Skill 2 (Clean Up) inflicts Bleed on coin 1 and extends Bleed Count by 3 on coin 2 (with a Damage Down proc on Heads Hit); Skill 3 (Lenticular Swirl) extends Bleed Count by 3 and applies Offense Level Down on the final coin. Damage bonuses unlock at 2+ Bleed Count on Skill 1 (+20%), 5+ on Skill 2 (+20%), and 9+ on Skill 3 (+40%) — prioritise hitting the Skill 3 threshold before using it. Her Support Passive (Kokuuntō) grants the entire team +10% damage on targets that have Bleed, and her Combat Passive boosts the fastest Speed ally's Bleed infliction by +1, making her a solid support pick in any Bleed composition.
