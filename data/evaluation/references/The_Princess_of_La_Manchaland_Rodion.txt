The Princess of La Manchaland Rodion is a Bloodfeast Support and Damage Dealer who distributes Blooming Thorn buffs to allies and unlocks a powerful multi-target alternate Skill 3 at 25+ Blooming Thorn. Skills 1 and 2 consume Bloodfeast each Combat Start to apply Blooming Thorn to herself and allies based on Bloodfeast consumed; Skill 3 (The Festival Will End) spreads Festive Fever and Blooming Thorn to a number of allies equal to the highest Resonance, and at 25+ Blooming Thorn activates the alternate Ascendant Don Quixote Hardblood Arts - The Finale — a 3 Atk Weight skill that deals +1% damage per 5 Bloodfeast consumed (max 50%) and applies even more Festive Fever. Her Clashable Counter (Don Quixote Hardblood Arts 15: Parasol) converts allies' Bleed into Bloodfeast and reduces Bleed Count, acting as a Bloodfeast generator and Bleed maintenance tool. Three combat passives together feed her Blooming Thorn — the Bloodfeast passive generates stacks from ally Bleed damage, Hardblood Thorn gains a Thorn whenever allies take Bleed damage or consume Bloodfeast, and "Blossom with Blood…" converts the highest-Bleed ally's Potency into Blooming Thorn at turn end — making her the nucleus of La Manchaland compositions.
