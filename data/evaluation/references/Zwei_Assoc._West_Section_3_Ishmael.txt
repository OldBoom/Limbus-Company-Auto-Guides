Zwei Assoc. West Section 3 Ishmael is a Tremor Tank who converts self Tremor Count into Defense Level Up and Coin Power to simultaneously survive and deal damage. Skill 1 and Skill 2 gain Aggro to their slots next turn and accumulate Defense Level Up from Tremor Count on self; Skill 3 (Ward) deals +70% damage when in Defensive Stance, scales Coin Power up to +4 from Defense Level Up, and on the final Coin dumps all self Tremor Count as Tremor on the target and triggers Tremor Burst. The Guard (Your Shield) is a Clashable Guard that generates 2 Defensive Stance per turn, adds +10 Tremor Count on use, and triggers Tremor Burst on Clash Win — meaning using the defense slot feeds directly into the Skill 3 damage loop. The Combat Passive (Shock-nullifying Greatsword) doubles Defense Level Up gained from Tremor Count when in Defensive Stance, and Emergency Protection applies Defense Level Up to whichever ally took the most damage between turns.
