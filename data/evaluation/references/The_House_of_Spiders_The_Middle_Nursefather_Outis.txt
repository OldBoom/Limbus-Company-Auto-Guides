The House of Spiders: The Middle Nursefather Outis is a Bleed and Burn hybrid Damage Dealer who accumulates The Middle — Grudge (Outis) stacks through ally damage intake and converts them into The Middle — Vengeance Tattoo — Great Sister — marks that strengthen her skills. All three base skills scale Coin Power from combined Burn + Bleed on target; Skill 2 and Skill 3 gain Unbreakable Coins equal to her Vengeance Tattoo count and — when her sword is at First or Second Seal Removed — inflict Burn equal to the base Bleed amount on each coin hit. Skill 3 (Stamp of Vengeance) scales Final Power off Vengeance Tattoo stacks and consumes all Grudge on Attack End; the alternate Skill 3 (Time to Unpack!) peels sword seals with four Unbreakable Coins applying Burn, Bleed, Stagger Threshold raise, and Envy Fragility. At 6+ Envy Resonance, **Counter: Rule Violation** fires **Stamp of Vengeance** or **Complete and Total Extermination** as a Counter at Combat Start — the defense slot is your burst tool, not just protection, and it also grants Shield scaled off missing HP. Her Support Passive (Someone Mess With the Family?) applies Damage Up and Protection to all Counter-equipped allies at Combat Start — doubling to Envy DMG Up and Protection for Envy allies.
