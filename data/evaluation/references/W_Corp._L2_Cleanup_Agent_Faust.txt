W Corp. L2 Cleanup Agent Faust is a utility-oriented Charge builder who generates Charge Count across all skills and spends it on control effects. Skill 1 (Energy Cycle) and Skill 2 (Leap) are efficient Charge stackers; Skill 2's second Coin consumes 3 Charge Count to inflict 4 Bind next turn. Skill 3 (Overcharge) gains Clash Power +2 at 5+ Charge Count and on the final Coin spends 5 Charge Count to inflict 2 Attack Power Down and 3 Paralyze next turn — making Skill 3 primarily a control tool rather than a damage finisher. The Combat Passive (Self-charging) passively generates 1 Charge Count per Attack End for both Faust and a random ally, while Radio Comms gives 1 Charge Count to the ally with the lowest max HP each turn — making her the primary Charge-sharing support in W Corp. compositions.
