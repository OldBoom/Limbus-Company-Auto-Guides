N Corp. Mittelhammer Don Quixote is a Tremor and Nails specialist who softens enemies with debuffs for follow-up burst from Skill 2. Skill 1 inflicts Tremor and conditionally applies Nails on Heads Hit; Skill 3 (Fanatical Judgement) applies Nails across three coins and — on Heads Hit at 5+ Nails — inflicts Attack Power Down and Paralyze next turn. Skill 2 (Enactment!) is the payoff: it triggers Tremor Burst on hit and deals +20% damage against targets with 5+ Nails, rewarding the Nails setup. The Combat Passive (Thou Shalt Hammer!) ties the two mechanics together — targets that have Nails receive +1 Tremor Count, and if this unit is in Fanatic state she also gains +1 Nails per hit. The second passive (Hammer of Zealotry) grants the Fanatic ally with the least SP +10% damage, rewarding low-SP play or deliberate SP-drain compositions.
