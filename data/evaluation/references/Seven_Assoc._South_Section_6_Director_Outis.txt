Seven Assoc. South Section 6 Director Outis is a Clash controller and debuffer who imposes -2 Clash Power on opponents in every clash and debuffs enemies with Defense Level Down and Offense Level Down to enable teammates. Skill 1 inflicts Paralyze next turn; Skill 2 (Field Command) inflicts Rupture Count and 2 Defense Level Down on each hit; Skill 3 (Exploit the Gap) unlocks Coin Power +2 at 7+ Rupture and on the third Coin inflicts Offense Level Down and applies Weakness Analyzed next turn. The Combat Passive (Relay Intelligence) gives the fastest ally +10% damage when attacking into a type weakness or fatal type, and her Support Passive (Grasping Vulnerabilities) grants any identity +10% damage when exploiting the same — making her a force-multiplier for type-advantage compositions rather than a personal damage identity.
