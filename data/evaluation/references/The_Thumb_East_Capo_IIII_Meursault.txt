The Thumb East Capo IIII Meursault is a Damage Dealer and Status Specialist who cycles through two phases of Tigermark ammunition. He starts with 12 Tigermark Rounds; while loaded, Tanglecleaver's coins become Unbreakable with +1 Power and +10% damage per Tigermark spent coin, and the Skill 3 triggers Tremor — Scorch conversion and Tremor Burst on the final coin. Once all Tigermark is exhausted — or after using a Defense Skill for the first time — he reloads 8 Savage Tigermark Rounds: all Base Attack coins become Unbreakable with +2 Power and +30% damage, and coin hits inflict +2 additional Burn Potency and Burn Count; Skill 3 is replaced by Savage Tigerslayer's Perfected Flurry of Blades with a 5-coin chain. Equip a defense skill once per encounter deliberately to trigger the Savage Tigermark reload even if Tigermark is not fully spent — plan when you switch to defense to unlock the upgraded kit. His passive (Savage Tiger's Lunge) benefits the fastest Speed ally who spends ammo, granting +(Speed difference × 3)% damage (max 15%) when faster than the target by 3 or more, making him a natural pair with The Thumb East Soldato II Sinclair.
