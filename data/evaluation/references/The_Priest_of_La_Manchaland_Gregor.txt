The Priest of La Manchaland Gregor is a Tank and Support who absorbs hits for the team while building Bloodied Hand stacks to power a missing-HP-scaling Skill 3. With 313 HP and a single stagger threshold at 80%, all three skills grant +3 Aggro next turn, drawing fire reliably; Skill 3 (The Unforgivable Sin) deals up to +90% bonus damage based on his own missing HP, heals 10% HP at Attack End, and unlocks further Coin Power or a final coin reuse by spending 10+ or 20+ excess Bloodied Hand above 10. Use **Counter: Faded Patience** at Combat Start each turn — above 40% HP it builds self-Bleed and activates Bleed up to 3 times; below 40% it consumes Bloodfeast to heal instead, feeding Skill 3's missing-HP scaling before the attack chain. The Combat Passive (Self-destructive Dedication) heals the lowest-SP ally each turn and nullifies one lethal hit per encounter. His Support Passive heals the lowest-HP ally 3 HP per hit (6 HP if the target had Bleed), and his Bloodfeast passive sustains the shared resource pool for allied La Manchaland identities.
