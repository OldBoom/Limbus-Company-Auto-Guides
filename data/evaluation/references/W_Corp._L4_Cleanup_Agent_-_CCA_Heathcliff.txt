W Corp. L4 Cleanup Agent - CCA Heathcliff is a Charge-scaling Tank and Damage Dealer who accumulates Charge Potency tiers to unlock compounding survivability and Skill 3 power. Skill 2 (Boost: Elbow Blade) generates Charge Barrier from Charge Potency each Combat Start and consumes Charge Count for Coin Power; Skill 3 (Rip Space - CCA Overdrive) converts all Coins to Unbreakable at 3+ Charge Potency or below 50% HP, spends 10 Charge Count for Coin Power +4, and on the final coin consumes additional Charge Count for bonus Slash damage proportional to Charge Potency. The key Defense Skill (CCA Overcharge) is a Clashable Guard with an Unbreakable Coin that, when Charge Count is below 15, grants Overcharge, removes the first Stagger Threshold once per encounter, and queues an extra Rip Space - CCA Overdrive next turn — making the guard slot a damage amplifier, not just protection. The Combat Passives convert Charge into Defense Level Up and generate Shield when below 50% HP, and the Support Passive grants Charge Barrier to the earliest-deployed ally who overflows their Charge Count cap.
