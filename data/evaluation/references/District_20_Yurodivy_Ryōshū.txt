District 20 Yurodivy Ryoshu is a Tremor Count builder and Burst support who accumulates self Tremor Count and transfers it to enemies. Skill 1 and Skill 2 both gain self Tremor Count and benefit from Clash Power bonuses when the target has 4–6 Tremor; Skill 2's third Coin consumes self Tremor Count and inflicts the same amount as Tremor Count on target. Skill 3 (Percussive Maintenance) consumes 8 Tremor Count on use for Coin Power +1 and gains further Coin Power if the target has 6+ Tremor, then triggers Tremor Burst on the final coin. The Combat Passive (The A.I.) grants the first-deployed ally +1 extra Tremor Count from their Skill or Coin effects twice per turn, and the Support Passive (N.O.) triggers an automatic Skill 1 follow-up against any Staggered enemy, adding a Tremor Burst on the last coin — making her an excellent Tremor enabler for the whole team.
