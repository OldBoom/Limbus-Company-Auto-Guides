Liu Assoc. South Section 4 Ishmael is a Damage Dealer in Burn teams who applies Burn and Burn Count to unlock a significant damage bonus on Skill 3. Skill 2's final coin inflicts 2 Burn Count when the target has 6+ Burn; Skill 3 (Inner Gate Elbow Strike) deals +50% damage when the target has both 6+ Burn and Burn Count, and on Attack End grants Plus Coin Boost next turn if the target is Staggered or defeated. Her Support Passive (Intense Blaze) grants the team +10% Blunt damage for every 3 Burn Count on target (max 30%), and her Combat Passive gives the slowest Speed ally +5% Blunt damage per 3 Burn Count (max 15%), making her a reliable Burn Count contributor in Burn compositions.
