Kurokumo Clan Wakashu Heathcliff is a Damage Dealer and Tank who builds Bleed stacks while accumulating defensive buffs (Defense Power Up, Cumulus Wall, Dark Cloud Blade) to power an extremely potent Clashable Counter. Skill 1 and Skill 2 stack Bleed, apply Offense Level Down and Slash Fragility respectively, and each generate Defense Power Up next turn; Skill 3's final coin inflicts 6 Bleed and deals up to +100% damage based on negative effect types, and grants Dark Cloud Blade on Clash Win and Cumulus Wall at combat start. The Counter (Rules of the Backstreets) is the payoff: if Kurokumo Clan Captain Ishmael is on the field, all its coins become Unbreakable and it gains Final Power +4 on Clash Lose; if Heathcliff has Cumulus Wall, all Reuse effects on Coin 1 activate unconditionally, and each additional trigger (Dark Cloud Blade, Battle Ready) adds another reuse. His Support Passive (Dark Cloud Blade) also fires Unopposed Attacks against any enemy that damages an ally — escalating to the full Counter if the ally dies or drops below 25% HP — making him a strong protector in Kurokumo Clan compositions alongside Captain Ishmael.
