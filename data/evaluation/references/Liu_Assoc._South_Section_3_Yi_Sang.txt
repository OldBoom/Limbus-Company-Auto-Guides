Liu Assoc. South Section 3 Yi Sang is a Support and Damage Dealer who combines Burn application with sustained SP healing for the team. Skills 1 and 2 inflict Burn with Coin Power scaling from Burn on target; Skill 2's final coin deals +5% per Burn (max 60%) and extends Burn Count at 6+, while Skill 3 (Flow of the Sword) heals 15 SP to the lowest-SP ally at Attack End and heals an additional ally if the target is killed or had 10+ Burn. Skill 3's final coin adds Wrath damage equal to Burn Potency (max 30) and reduces Burn Count by 2, converting stacked Burn duration into burst damage. The Combat Passive (Inner Ardor) heals the lowest-SP ally every turn — with bonus healing for Burn-inflicting allies — and the Support Passive (Liu Defensive Square) heals the two lowest-SP allies at Turn End, with stronger healing for those whose skills apply Burn.
