Liu Assoc. South Section 4 Director Rodion is a Status Specialist and Damage Dealer who spreads Burn across multiple enemies and amplifies the whole team once all enemies are Burning. Skill 2's final coin (at 6+ Burn Count on target) applies 3 Burn to two enemies with the least Burn — the main multi-target Burn setter; Skill 3 (Pinpoint Blitz) deals +30% damage when the target has both 6+ Burn and Burn Count, and the final coin deals Wrath Affinity damage equal to Burn Potency (max 30) while reducing Burn Count by 2. Her Support Passive (Burning Passion) adds +10% damage per 6 Burn Potency on target (max 30%) and at Combat Start applies Wrath DMG Up to allies ranked by Wrath Resonance when all enemies have 6+ Burn Potency, with Attack Power Up additionally for Liu Association Fixers.
