The Ring Fauvist Docent Rodion is a Status Specialist and Damage Dealer who spreads Bleed, Sinking, Mauled Color — Rouge, and Mauled Color — Bleu while scaling damage and Clash/Final Power off how many negative effect types sit on the target. Skills 1 and 2 stack Mauled Colors and varied debuffs; Skill 3 (Le Fauvisme — Déchaînement Brutal) transforms into Burst Forth the Pulsing Crimson under Your Skin when the target has 6+ negative effect types, unleashing an Unfocused Volley of Unbreakable coins for the main payoff. Her Clashable Counter Trimming the Medium can auto-activate as Critique when she lacks Réflexion and the target has 3+ negative effects, while Critique reuses coins up to twice based on debuff diversity and inflicts Touche Hardie on the third coin. Combat passive Le Fauvisme — Couleurs Sauvages applies Palette Vibrante at Combat End and can grant Masque de Loup after enough Palette/Critique/Burst use; Support passive Nice and Slow, Don't Let It Break… buffs the earliest-deployed ally with extra Bleed or Sinking Potency keyed to Lust Resonance and grants Ring allies random Offense or Defense Level Up at Combat Start.
