Seven Assoc. South Section 4 Heathcliff is a Status Specialist who focuses on building Rupture Count to enable Rupture-scaling teammates. Skill 2 (The Wrap-Up) inflicts Defense Level Down and Rupture Count on hit, with an additional Count on Clash Win; Skill 3 (Forensics) extends Rupture Count across multiple coins and, on Kill, spreads Rupture Count to the enemy with the least. The Combat Passives (Instinctive Read and Connect the Dots) each add +1 more Rupture infliction when hitting with a damage type the enemy is Fatal to, stacking additional bonuses in Fixer-heavy parties — best paired with Seven Assoc. South Section 4 Faust, who converts his Rupture Count buildup into high damage output.
