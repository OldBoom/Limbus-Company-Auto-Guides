N Corp. E.G.O::Contempt, Awe Ryoshu is a dual-status Bleed + Tremor Damage Dealer who inflicts Gaze (Ryoshu) stacks to trigger increasingly powerful alternate Skill 3 variants. Standard Skill 3 (Awe, Contempt) deals +1% damage per combined Bleed + Tremor on target (max 40%) and gains +2 potency from both if the target has Gaze; once 7 Gaze accumulate across all enemies (or any Contempt is present), Skill 3 replaces with Cascading Gaze of Awe Underneath Contempt — a 3 Atk Weight Unclashable skill with BP 14 and CP +14 that deals +200% damage against single targets and consumes all Gaze and Contempt on hit. She also carries a unique Skill 4 (I Shall Fire / Anytime) that scales off Torn Memory and Poise, spending SP for massive single-target critical damage against the main target while striking sub-targets simultaneously. Her Support Passives inflict Gaze on any hit or counterattack and passively spread Bleed and Tremor through the first-deployed ally, making her one of the most offensively loaded E.G.O identities in the roster.
