Devyat' Assoc. North Section 3 Sinclair is a Damage Dealer who manages two parallel resources: Courier Trunk — Sinclair stacks on self and Rupture stacks on the enemy, both contributing Coin Power (+1 per 10 Courier Trunk or per 6 Rupture, max 2). Skills 1 and 2 build Courier Trunk through hits — doubling the gain rate below 15 stacks — and inflate Rupture Count; once the target reaches 15+ Rupture and 3+ Rupture Count, each skill's On Hit Rupture infliction converts instead to Defense Level Down. Skill 3 (Poludnitsa) is the payoff: at 15+ Rupture the final coin swaps to +4 Coin Power, and the skill deals +4% damage per Courier Trunk on self (max 120%), rewarding high Trunk counts before commitment. He opens tougher fights stronger than most identities — Trunk and proportional Shield ramp quickly — and peaks as stacks approach the 15+ maintenance band where Poludnitsa's scaling and Defense Level Down conversion turn him into a short-window burst threat; plan to retreat via **W-wait, I need to do some maintenance…!** and Strategic R&R Mode once allies have gathered enough setup to take over, since **Upon Retreat** (A Moment, Please…) applies Clash Power Up to the team and rejoining halves Courier Trunk once per encounter. Excellent as an early-encounter breaker, not as a long-fight anchor.
