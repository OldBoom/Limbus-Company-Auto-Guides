Lobotomy E.G.O::Red Sheet Sinclair is a Rupture and Talisman support who inflicts Rupture to generate Talisman stacks and then redistributes them to boost allied Rupture output. Skill 1 (Magnify Wound) inflicts 2 Rupture and gains 1 Talisman on a Heads Hit when the target has 5+ Rupture; Skill 2 (Proliferating Talismans) is the primary Talisman builder, gaining 1 per coin and an extra stack if the target has 3+ Rupture; Skill 3 (Rupturing Talisman) transfers 5 Talisman to the target and inflicts +1 Rupture Count on the second Coin if the target already has Talisman. The Combat Passive (Wishing Talisman) grants the ally with the highest current HP +1 bonus Rupture Potency with every attack and Counter Skill, and the Support Passive (Unfixing Talismans) feeds Talisman back to Sinclair whenever any ally hits a target that has Rupture — making him the Talisman engine of Rupture-focused compositions.
