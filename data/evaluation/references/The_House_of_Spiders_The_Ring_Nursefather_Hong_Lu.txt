Ring Nursefather Hong Lu is a Support and Damage Dealer — a Bleed-focused identity that builds Corpus Ingredient stacks. Gains Corpus Ingredient Potency every time 10 cumulative Corpus Ingredient Count is consumed; reaching Potency 2 unlocks Artwork: Tibia, which scales attacks based on Corpus Ingredient Potency, grants bonus speed and damage, and replaces the Base Skill set. Manages Tableau stacks that are spent when Ring-faction allies use skills and at turn end; when stacks run out, one of Base Skills is replaced by an alternate version that applies a faction-wide buff to Speed, Clash Power, and damage. Skills gain bonus damage for every type of negative status effect on the target, and Final Power scales with Bleed on the target.
