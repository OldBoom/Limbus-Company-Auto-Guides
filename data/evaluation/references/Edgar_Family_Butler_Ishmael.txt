Edgar Family Butler Ishmael is a Status Specialist who applies Sinking and layered debuffs when outpacing targets in Speed. All three skills gain Coin Power +1 when faster than the target; Skill 1 builds Poise and inflicts Sinking on the final coin, with Haste gained on Crit; Skill 2 inflicts Defense Level Down and Sinking, and on Critical Hit applies Bind next turn; Skill 3 (Restraining Technique) locks down the target with Sinking Count, Bind, and Slash Fragility on its final coin. The Combat Passive (Deep Cleaning) amplifies all negative effect Potency by +1 when Critically Hitting slower targets (3 times per turn), and the Loyal Supporter passive rewards the fastest Speed ally with +1% bonus damage per Sinking Potency on target (max 20%).
