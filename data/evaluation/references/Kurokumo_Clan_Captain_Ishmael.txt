Kurokumo Clan Captain Ishmael is a Damage Dealer and Status Specialist who scales off both Bleed and the total number of negative effect types on the target. Stack Bleed through Skill 1 and Skill 2, and layer Offense Level Down (Skill 1 Coin 2) and Slash Fragility (Skill 2 Coin 1 on Heads Hit) to maximize the negative-effect-type count for the Skill 2 (+50% max) and Skill 3 (+100% max) damage multipliers; Skill 2 also extends Bleed Count and grants Slash Power Up. Save Skill 3 for when the target already has several debuff types — the final coin procs Bleed on the target 3 times before reducing Bleed Count by 3, dealing a burst of Bleed damage independent of Coin flips. Her Support Passives give Dark Cloud resource to adjacent Kurokumo Clan allies and boost the fastest-Speed ally's Bleed Potency and Count by 1 on skill and coin effects.
