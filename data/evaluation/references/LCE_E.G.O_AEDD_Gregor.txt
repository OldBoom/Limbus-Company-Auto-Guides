LCE E.G.O::AEDD Gregor is a Charge-stacking Damage Dealer who converts Charge Potency tiers (gained by consuming cumulative Charge Count) into compounding offensive bonuses on all three skills. Skill 1 and Skill 2 grant Charge Count per hit, scale Coin Power at 3+ and 5+ Charge, and unlock Unbreakable Coins; Skill 3 (High-voltage Current Discharge) activates as the stronger Maximum Output — Ultra High-voltage Current Discharge whenever 3+ Charge is present, adding a 3 Atk Weight Unclashable skill that spends excess Charge Count for bonus damage. **Counter: This E.G.O Gift's Defensive Capability is Uniquely Superior** grants High — voltage Exoskeleton at Combat Start — use the defense slot to convert Charge Barrier into the exoskeleton buff before committing Skill 3. The Combat Passive (Undying Persistence) converts any Charge Barrier into High-voltage Exoskeleton, boosts Min/Max Speed at 3+ Charge, and grants a one-time Self-charge revival when HP drops to 0. His second passive redistributes up to 8 Charge Count to earlier-deployed allies each Combat End, and heals them via Charge consumption tracked through the Support Passive (Defibrillation), making him a Charge-sharing support for teams built around W Corp. or Charge archetypes.
