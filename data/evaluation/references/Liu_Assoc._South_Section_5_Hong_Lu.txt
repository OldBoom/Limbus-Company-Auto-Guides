Liu Assoc. South Section 5 Hong Lu is a Support identity in Burn teams who sustains team SP and extends Burn Count. Skill 1 heals 3 SP on use regardless of combat outcome; Skill 2 (Flowing Flame) inflicts Burn and extends Burn Count, with additional Count at 30+ SP and even more at 45+ SP; Skill 3 (Crimson Blaze Fist) heals 6 SP on Heads Hit and raises his Stagger Threshold by 30% of damage dealt when hitting a target with 6+ Burn Count. His Combat Passives grant +1 Burn Count to his own skill and coin effects at 30+ SP, and give the ally with the most SP the same bonus — keeping SP high is both his contribution and his mechanical requirement.
