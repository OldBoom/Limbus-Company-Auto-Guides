Firefist Office Survivor Gregor is a Status Specialist and Damage Dealer who manages District 12 Fuel to spread Burn and escalate personal damage. He starts with 100 Fuel; while above 50 stacks, Fuel-spending coins deal +15% damage, but at 50 or fewer the resource converts to Overheated Fuel, raising that bonus to +30% and raising Skill 3 (Firefist)'s damage cap from 50% to 100% — intentionally burning down to Overheated for peak burst is the goal. Skill 2 spreads Burn to two additional enemies with the least Burn on every coin hit, and Skill 3 also scales Base Power with Burn on target (+1 per 6 Burn, max +3). Use **Counter: I have to keep going for big sis** at Combat Start to grant 100 District 12 Fuel before the attack chain — the defense slot is your fuel refill and setup turn, not passive protection. His Support Passive deals up to +0.3% damage per Fuel cumulatively spent this encounter and, on Stagger or kill of a Burning enemy, spreads Burn Count to two enemies with the least.
