Magic Bullet Outis is a Status Specialist in Burn teams. She accumulates Magic Bullet stacks via Skill 2, Skill 3, and the Defense defensive skill, then spends them on Skill 3 to fire a powerful shot whose Atk Weight, Base Power, and Dark Flame infliction all scale with current Magic Bullet count. Dark Flame is a unique Burn variant inflicted by her skills; Skill 2 and Skill 3 apply Dark Flame based on Magic Bullet Potency, while Skill 1 only applies 1 flat. Her passive grants Coin Power and damage bonuses based on Burn Potency on the enemy.
