Oufi Assoc. South Section 3 Heathcliff is a Tremor Decay specialist who builds Tremor Potency on enemies and converts it into Tremor — Decay via Amplitude Conversion for massive burst damage. Skills 1 and 2 inflict Tremor and stack Coin Power from Tremor on target; Skill 3 (Execution Sentencing) on the final Coin triggers Amplitude Conversion when the target has 20+ combined Tremor + Tremor Count, converting all Tremor into Tremor — Decay and simultaneously triggering Tremor Burst via the Combat Passive (Grave Attendance). Grave Attendance also adds +10% damage per 6 Tremor Potency on target (max 30%), compounding with the conversion window. The second passive (Verify Obligation Fulfillment) grants the fastest ally Clash Power +1 on all Tremor-applying Skills. His Counter (Do Not Obstruct) inflicts +1 Tremor Count on any attacker that strikes him while he has Shield, creating a passive Tremor-spreading effect from being targeted.
