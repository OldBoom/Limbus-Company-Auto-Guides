Heishou Pack - Wu Branch Adept Yi Sang is a Speed-gated cavalry Damage Dealer who stacks Linebreaker by outrunning enemies to unlock a devastating multi-target alternate Skill 3. All three attack skills gain Coin Power from combined Rupture + Tremor on target and deal bonus damage when Yi Sang's Speed exceeds the target's; Skill 2 grants Haste next turn, and Skill 3 (Cavalry's Vanguard Charge) converts all coins to Unbreakable at 3+ Linebreaker, also scaling off Shield HP on self. At 5 Linebreaker, Skill 3 activates as the alternate Obsidian Stormrider's Crescent Decapitator — a 3 Atk Weight Unclashable skill that applies Commanding Cry to all allies. **Guard: Preparation Afore the Charge.** grants 3 Strider — Wu — at Combat Start — use the defense slot to enter Strider before charging so Linebreaker and double Tremor Burst triggers come online faster. The Combat Passive (Obsidian Stormrider) gains Linebreaker when outrunning targets, triggering double Tremor Bursts, or winning Clashes with Strider — Wu —, and converts Shield HP into Defense Level. His combo passive specifically buffs Family Hierarch Candidate Ishmael, making Yi Sang a key pillar of H Corp. and Heishou Pack team compositions.
