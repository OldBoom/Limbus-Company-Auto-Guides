Seven Assoc. South Section 4 Faust is a Status Specialist who stacks Rupture and then cashes out with Skill 3 for scaling damage. Skill 1 applies Rupture with Coin Power scaling from existing Rupture on target; Skill 2 (Dissect Target) extends Rupture Count on Clash Win and applies the unique Weakness Analyzed debuff on the final coin when the target has 6+ Rupture. Skill 3 (Profiling) deals +10% damage per 3 Rupture (max 50%), extends Rupture Count on Clash Win, and grants Haste and Slash Power Up on Stagger or kill. The Combat Passive (Before the Tea Cools…) converts Rupture on the target into Poise whenever Faust attacks with a damage type the enemy is Weak or Fatal to, so type-advantage planning matters for sustaining her Poise alongside Rupture output.
