R Corp. 4th Pack Rhino Meursault is a Charge-and-Bleed Tank who accumulates Charge Count to boost speed and damage, then cashes out with Skill 3 (Rhino Ram) to inflict Bleed Count. Skill 1 builds 2 Charge Count per use plus 2 on hit, with a Final Power bonus at 10+ Charge Count and a speed-gated +25% damage at 7+ Speed; Skill 2 adds Bleed Count on hits and gains 4 Charge Count. Skill 3 spends up to 5 Charge Count across three coins to inflict Bleed Count, with Coin Power +2 at 10+ Charge Count. The Combat Passive (Activate rrR-#4 Suit Pressurization) converts Charge Count into Max Speed at turn end (up to +6) and grants 3 Aggro at 5+ Charge — making him an aggro-drawing frontline tank who accelerates himself each turn. His second passive (Maneuver Training) shares speed boosts to the ally with the most Charge Count, enabling Charge-synergy team compositions.
