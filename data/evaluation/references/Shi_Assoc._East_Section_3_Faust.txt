Shi Assoc. East Section 3 Faust is a Poise and Bleed Damage Dealer who has two primary game loops: one involves accumulating Arrow — Shi stacks, entering Ready to Loose state via the Evade defense skill, and then unleashing the unclashable Bow's Glimmer at Combat Start — which scales from +48% up to +444% critical damage at maximum Target Aim stacks and second is staying in base form and fighting as a regular Bleed and Poise id. Skill 1 and Skill 2 build Poise and Bleed Count; all three attack skills scale Coin Power and damage from the combined total of Poise on self and Bleed on target. The key mechanic is the Defense Skill (Unfold Bowblade): when this unit has Ready to Loose and uses the defense slot without attacking, she gains 1 Target Aim next turn, stacking up to 4; each Bow's Glimmer use consumes all Target Aim. The Combat Passive (Snipe - Archery) fires Bow's Glimmer automatically at Combat Start when Ready to Loose is active, amplifying the replaced skill's base stats based on which skill slot was used. Her Support Passive (Aiming for the Bullseye) applies 1 Pierce DMG Up each turn to the ally who inflicted the most Bleed Potency, making her a natural pairing with Bleed-heavy teammates.
