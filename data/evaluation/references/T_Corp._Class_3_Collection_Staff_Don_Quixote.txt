T Corp. Class 3 Collection Staff Don Quixote is a Tremor Tank and sustain identity who accumulates Borrowed Time charges, generates heavy Aggro, and applies Time Moratorium to stop enemies. Skill 1 builds Tremor Count, generates Aggro, and gains Clash Power from Tremor on target; Skill 2 steals Tremor from enemies and triggers Tremor Burst on the third Coin; Skill 3 (I Command Thee, Halt!) consumes 10 Tremor Count for Coin Power +2, triggers Amplitude Conversion into Tremor — Chain when the target is slower than this unit, and on the final Coin consumes 10 Tremor from the target to apply 2 Time Moratorium. Use **Counter: I Shall Summon the Time** while holding Aggro — it generates 4 Borrowed Time at Turn End and retaliates with 3 Tremor against attackers, making the defense slot part of her sustain and crowd-control loop. Her Combat Passive (Golden Time - Extracting) provides a one-time full emergency heal and recovery when below 20% HP, and grants extra Aggro each time Borrowed Time expires — making her the frontline anchor of Tremor teams.
