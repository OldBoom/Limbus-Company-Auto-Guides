The Manager of La Manchaland Don Quixote is a Damage Dealer who accumulates Bloodfeast and Hardblood to unlock a powerful alternate Variant Sancho Hardblood Arts skill set. Standard skills consume Bloodfeast to gain Hardblood and inflict Bleed with heal-on-hit; the Variant Arts replacements feature Unbreakable Coins, scale Atk Weight from Hardblood, and deal damage proportional to both Bleed on target and total Bloodfeast consumed — Variant Skill 2 caps at +25% and +75%, and Skill 3 (I Shall Impale) at +50% and +100%. Killing with the ultimate Ascendant version (La Sangre) refunds half its Hardblood cost and on kill spreads 5 Bleed and Bleed Count to all enemies. Her Support Passive gives Damage Up to the lowest-HP ally based on missing HP (stronger for Bloodfiend allies), rewarding her alongside the rest of the La Manchaland family.
