Blade Lineage Mentor Meursault is a Tank and Damage Dealer who converts clash losses and missing HP into damage. Build Poise with Skill 1 and Skill 2 — Coin Power increases at 5+ and 7+ Poise respectively — then commit Skill 3 (Yield My Flesh) when winning the clash: it deals up to +75% damage based on Poise Potency on Critical Hits and scales with missing HP; on a Clash Lose it automatically fires the Counter (To Claim Their Bones), which carries the same critical and HP scaling. **Counter: To Claim Their Bones** is the defense-slot payoff — after a lost Skill 3 clash it fires from the guard slot with the same crit and missing-HP scaling, so plan defense as part of the finisher rotation, not only emergency protection. The Combat Passive (In Memoriam) accelerates Poise gain once three or more allies fall, and the passive (Swordplay of the Homeland) lets him deal +15% damage on Critical Hits for the Blade Lineage ally with the most Poise. His Support Passive (Swordplay of the Homeland) nullifies one lethal hit per encounter and shares 1 Poise Potency and Count to the two allies with the least Poise each time he gains Poise, making him the backbone of Blade Lineage Poise compositions.
