The Thumb East Soldato II Sinclair is a Status Specialist and Support who applies Tremor and Burn while resupplying ammunition for allied ammo-using identities. He begins with 15 Scorch Propellant Ammo; Skill 3 (Thruster Compression) spends 1 Ammo per coin and at 3+ Ammo converts all coins to Unbreakable with +30% damage, triggering Tremor Burst up to three times on the final coin; each Ammo spent also grants Damage Up equal to the amount spent next turn (max 3). Skill 2 (Respect the Thumb) deals +10% per 4 (Burn + Tremor) on target (max 30%), inflicts Burn Count when the target has 10+ Tremor — Scorch, and triggers Tremor Burst; the Guard (Ferrous Guard) inflicts 2 Tremor on attackers while Sinclair has Shield. His two Support Passives both resupply the earliest-deployed ammo-using ally when their ammo drops below half — Ammunition Tribute by spending his own Scorch Propellant to match what that ally spent, and Reloader by restoring half their maximum ammo — making him an essential pair for The Thumb East Capo IIII Meursault.
