Cinq Assoc. East Section 3 Don Quixote is a Damage Dealer and Status Specialist whose skills gain Coin Power based on Speed advantage over the target (Coin Power +1 per 2 Speed difference, max 2), so prioritise matching her against slower enemies to unlock full flip strength. The Combat Passive (Virescent Pyrojade) automatically applies Nerve Strike — Don Quixote to the last target hit by a Base Attack Skill each turn and converts sustained Poise into Haste at Turn End; Skill 3's final coin deals Critical damage equal to (Poise + Burn)% on target (max 50%) and inflicts 3 Defense Level Down when the target has Nerve Strike. Skill 2 gains additional Poise Count on Clash Win scaled to Nerve Strike stacks on the target, creating a Poise-sustain loop the longer she fights the same enemy. Her Support Passive grants the fastest Speed ally Haste on Clash Win against Burn or Unique Burn targets, scaling further at 20+ Burn Potency.
