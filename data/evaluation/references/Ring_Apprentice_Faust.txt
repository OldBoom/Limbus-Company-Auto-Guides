Ring Apprentice Faust is a Damage Dealer — a Bleed-focused identity that builds Corpus Ingredient stacks. Gains Corpus Ingredient Potency every time 10 cumulative Corpus Ingredient Count is consumed using Skill 2, Skill 3, or the Assist Defense Counter in Flow State. Artwork: Fascia is gained after reaching Corpus Ingredient Potency 2, scaling attacks based on Corpus Ingredient Potency. The unit begins the encounter in Iron Maiden state and, after gaining Artwork: Fascia, transitions to Flow State for higher speed and bonus damage, gaining a new set of Base Skills.
