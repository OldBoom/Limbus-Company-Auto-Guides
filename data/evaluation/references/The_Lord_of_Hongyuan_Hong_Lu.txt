The Lord of Hongyuan Hong Lu is a Support and Damage Dealer who commands the Heishou Pack from the front line, buffing allies at Combat Start with Defense Power Up, Gluttony DMG Up on Gluttony-skill allies, and Resonance-scaled Slash Power Up on Heishou identities via his Counter. His Combat Passive (The Heishou Lord) gains Leash for each Heishou Pack teammate, heals SP when Heishou units fall, and commands returning allies to fire Unopposed Skill 1—or Heishou/Jia Skill 3 twice per encounter. Tarnished Blood (Embrace the Tarnished Blood…) grants a once-per-encounter lethal nullify, stacks +3% skill damage per stack, and can apply Lordsguard to allies before unopposed hits; Life from Death further powers his Lonesome Stand Skill 3 variant. Defense usage: **Counter: Heishou Keenclaw** applies Slash Power Up to Resonance-scaled Heishou allies at Combat Start; **Guard: Lordsguard** is personal Assist Defense with +1 Clash Power vs targets with 3+ negative effect types. If he skips defense before an unopposed hit, Tarnished Blood applies Lordsguard to allies instead — alternate between self-guard and attack turns depending on whether allies need protection. Personal damage scales off Rupture + Poise and Heishou Bolus Contamination; Skill 3 branches include the unclashable Answer Me, Heishou Packs finisher and combo-gated paths that heal and buff Heishou allies on kill. Build around Heishou Pack and Jia Family Resonance for full command and buff value.
