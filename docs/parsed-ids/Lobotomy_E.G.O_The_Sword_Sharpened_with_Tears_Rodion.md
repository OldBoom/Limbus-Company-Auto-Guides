### The Sword Sharpened with Tears - !ALERT: E.G.O GEAR SYNC THRESHOLD EXCEEDED!

**(×3)**

When in Blessing state… ; Take -(SP / 2)% HP damage from attacks (max 20%) ; - Combat Start: at 3+ Protecting Sword, gain 1 Protection ; - Combat Start: at 5 Protecting Sword, gain 2 Protection and 1 Clash Power Up instead ; ; When in Despair state… ; - Deal +(-SP/2)% damage with Base Skills (max 20%) ; - Combat Start: at 3+ Piercing Sword, gain 1 Damage Up ; - Combat Start: at 5 Piercing Sword gain 2 Damage Up and 1 Power Up instead

**(×5 Owned)**
